Welcome to the Dream World

  • Is the 3rd sub-unit of NCT
  • This sub-unit consists of 7 members: Mark, Renjun, Jeno, Haechan, Jaemin, Chenle, Jisung

  • NCT stands for Neo Culture Technology
  • NCT Fandom's Name: NCTzen (meaning that they are citizens of NCT)
